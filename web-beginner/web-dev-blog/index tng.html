Hottest Jobs in the 24th Century #2: Captain's Assistant

By XO William T. Riker

Date: 43657.0

There is a simple reason why Captain's Assistant is such a hot job right now. Put simply, what Captain's Assistants do is create the environment for a pleasant captain experience. If you proceed from the notion that every person who engages with the Captain would prefer a great experience, then you can say that everyone who engages with the Captain would prefer that the Captain's Assistant has done a great job. Starfleet of course knows this, so they hire Captain's Assistants to ensure their Captains provide great experiences.
